Q: How do I use DADIP?

Q: Can I use any different File-Formats or does it have to be an Excel-File?
A: Currently only Excel Files (xlsx or csv) can be processed by our algorithm, but maybe there will be more options in the future...
Q: I get an error message everytime I upload a File even though the File-Type is correct, what could be the reason?
