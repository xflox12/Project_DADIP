Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How do I use DADIP?
A: There is a manual on the Startpage which explains step by step what you have to do. You can also click here to see the manual.

Q: Can I use any different File-Formats or does it have to be an Excel-File?
A: Currently only Excel Files (xlsx or csv) can be processed by our algorithm, but maybe there will be more options in the future...

Q: I get an error message everytime I upload a File even though the File-Type is correct, what could be the reason?
A: Make sure you only upload 1 file at a time and also there is a Filesize limit of 10mb per file.

Q: Will the uploaded Data be stored or used for any additional aspects?
A: Your uploaded Data is being stored on the Server for functional reasons. But your data is only being used for Fraud Analysis and nothing else!

Q: Your project is so cool, how did you manage to build it in such a short period of time?
A: Thanks for your compliment! We had very good supervisors who helped us out when we were stuck at any point during the project :-)
